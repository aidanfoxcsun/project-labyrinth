
All Tiles 32x32

Ground:
(on Mockup 17-20)
Ground is made out of 4 Tiles, edge (17), border parts (18,19), middle part (20)

Walls:
Wall Edge is build out of 4 Tiles (on Mockup 1,2,8,9)
Wall middle Part can be repeated (on Mockup 3,10 or 15,16)

Jail:
(on Mockup 4-7 & 11-14)
Jail can be repeated, there are two different adapted wall parts, 
one variant to be repeated with normal wall middle part and one variant to place another jail beside

Holes:
One narrow hole (33-35), you can stretch it in length, its made out of 3 tiles and middle (34) is repeatable
One Big Hole set (21-32), you can build multiple big holes out of it, with edges and repeatable parts

Rocks are single Assets with transparency 
Doors are single Spritesheets with animations
